About

Built from scratch,
by hand.

We're VinnieBuilds — a carpenter, maker, and designer team based in Aotearoa New Zealand. We've spent years working with our hands, learning that the best things in a home are the ones made with care and built to outlast trends.

Whether it's a small wooden trinket for your shelf or a custom dining table for your family, every piece we make starts with a conversation and ends with something you'll keep forever.

Based in our workshop in New Zealand, we work with sustainably sourced timbers and a whole lot of love for the craft.
